Cripps Pink Apples

What are these bad boys?

Developed as a hybrid between the Lady Williams and the Golden Deilicious.

Originally bred by John Cripps at the Stoneville Research Station in Western Australia.

Known and loved by Jamie Brawns worldwide.

Key properties

  • firm
  • long-storing
  • sweet
  • low storage-scald
  • tart

Biggest European Markets (July 2022)

  1. Germany: 41,103 tonnes
  2. United Kingdom: 36,420 tonnes
  3. France: 24,778 tonnes
